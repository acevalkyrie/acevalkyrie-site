About AceValkyrie

My name is Levi Zelley, and I'm an aspiring full-stack developer based in Michigan. I got my start on front-end work in 2015, reverse engineering simple static web pages to impress my friends. I discovered a passion for HTML and CSS right then, and I've worked JavaScript, Python, C#, and C++ under my belt in the years after.

Nearly everything I learned came from passion and independent research over the years, with a few supplementary college courses in coding and UX at Mott Community College. Now you'll find me making web apps and hobby sites for any fun and interesting niche I spot unfilled, perpetually striving to push my skills and knowledge base just a little more.

When I'm not churning out silly sites for my friends, you can expect to find me reading, writing, working my way through yet another playthrough of Mass Effect, or walking the streets of downtown Grand Blanc.
